About PlaceMe

PlaceMe is a comprehensive college placement cell software designed to revolutionize the recruitment process. We bridge the gap between ambitious students and leading employers, facilitating seamless connections and fostering career growth.

Our platform offers a suite of powerful tools and features to support students, placement officers, and recruiters throughout the entire placement journey. From resume building to interview scheduling, we're committed to enhancing every step of the process.
